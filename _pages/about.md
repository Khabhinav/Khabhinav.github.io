I am a B.Tech student in Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi. My work spans machine learning, reinforcement learning, robotics, AI safety, and systems.

I have worked as a Research Intern at SRI International, where I fine-tuned large language models for portfolio management using preference-based reinforcement learning methods such as DPO and GRPO. I have also interned with Wipro AI, Lab45 on deep learning and NLP systems for insurance claim processing.

My current academic work includes semantically safe navigation with vision-language models, backdoor robustness for open-vocabulary object detectors, reinforcement learning in simulated control environments, robotic manipulation, and Linux kernel development.
