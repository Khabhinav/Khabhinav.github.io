Built a byte-level byte-pair encoding (BPE) tokenizer and decoder-only language model for a Hindi corpus from basic PyTorch modules, including rotary positional embeddings (RoPE) and grouped-query attention (GQA).

Code: [GitHub](https://github.com/Khabhinav/HindiLM).
