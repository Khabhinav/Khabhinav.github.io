Built a byte-level byte-pair encoding (BPE) tokenizer and decoder-only Hindi language model from basic PyTorch modules, with rotary positional embeddings (RoPE) and grouped-query attention (GQA). Achieved a compression ratio of 4.56, long-tail score of 0.38, and 2.42 bits per character.

Code: [GitHub](https://github.com/Khabhinav/HindiLM).
