Built a hierarchical planning system combining a vision-language model (VLM) for high-level decision-making over MDP options with a DWA-based low-level controller. Improved semantic safety in navigation by identifying risk-inducing objects as additional visual context and benchmarking VLM performance in uncertain environments.

Undergraduate thesis: [view thesis](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1YDo8a117sccprqWSJaHE4-YgctgOKO83/view?usp=sharing).
